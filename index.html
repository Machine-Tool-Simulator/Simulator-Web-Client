Watch the videos and learn how to use a lathe!

Turning on the Control

This video demonstrates the steps to turn on the control for the lathe.

To proceed, touch any of the buttons on the control to the right to active the control.

Setting the RPM

This video demonstrates the steps to set the RPM for the lathe to a desired number.

To proceed, set the spindle RPM to 1000 by pressing "Spin Speed," typing in a number (1000), and selecting "Inc Set" by following the steps from the video.

Turning on the Spindle

This video demonstrates the steps to turn the spindle on for the lathe.

To proceed, turn the the spindle on by selected the "FWD" button on the control to the right.

Turning off the Spindle

This video demonstrates the steps to turn the spindle off for the lathe.

To proceed, turn the spindle off by selecting the "OFF" button on the control to the right.
